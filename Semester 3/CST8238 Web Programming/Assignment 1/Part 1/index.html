About Us

ALOW is a charity organization based in Ottawa, Canada. Though our staff is small, we work to serve much of Ontario as well as Quebec, Manitoba and the Maritime provinces.

Through hard work and dedication, we hope one day to expand beyond Canada to the US, Europe, and the rest of the world.
